What am I looking at?

The Smith chart is the complex plane of Γ = Γr + jΓi. Every passive load with R ≥ 0 lands inside (or on) the unit circle.

  • Center — matched load, Γ = 0, no reflection.
  • Right rim — open circuit (Γ ≈ +1).
  • Left rim — short circuit (Γ ≈ −1).
  • |Γ| — radius from center; larger means worse match.
  • VSWR — (1+|Γ|)/(1−|Γ|); 1 is perfect, ∞ is total reflection.
Formulas used here

z = ZL / Z₀
Γ = (z − 1) / (z + 1)
ZL = Z₀ (1 + Γ) / (1 − Γ)
VSWR = (1 + |Γ|) / (1 − |Γ|)
RL = −20 log₁₀(|Γ|) dB
ML = −10 log₁₀(1 − |Γ|²) dB
